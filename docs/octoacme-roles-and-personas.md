# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on creating intuitive, user-centered experiences. They conduct user research, create prototypes, and collaborate closely with Product Managers, Developers, and stakeholders to ensure the product is both functional and delightful to use.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Define user flows and information architecture
- Collaborate with PM/PdM on feature requirements and user stories
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Iterate designs based on user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support adoption
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with PM and engineering teams
- Usability test findings and user research insights
- Design specifications and style guides
- Prototype walkthroughs and feedback sessions

### Example Interactions
- Works with **Product Managers** to translate user needs into design solutions
- Collaborates with **Developers** during implementation to ensure design feasibility and quality
- Shares insights with **Data Analysts** to understand user behavior patterns
- Presents designs to **Sponsors/Executive Stakeholders** for alignment on vision

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers bridge development and operations, focusing on automation, reliability, and efficient delivery pipelines. They build and maintain infrastructure, CI/CD systems, and work closely with Developers to enable rapid, safe deployments.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and automation
- Manage cloud infrastructure and deployment environments
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Optimize build times and deployment processes
- Collaborate with Developers on infrastructure requirements
- Ensure system reliability, scalability, and performance

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and automated deployments
- Minimize downtime and deployment-related incidents
- Improve developer productivity through automation

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure change notifications and deployment schedules
- Post-incident reviews and reliability metrics
- Capacity planning and performance reports
- Runbooks and operational documentation

### Example Interactions
- Partners with **Developers** to optimize build and deployment workflows
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on deployment schedules and risks
- Works with **Security Leads** to implement security controls in infrastructure
- Reports system health metrics to **Sponsors/Executive Stakeholders**

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads identify, assess, and mitigate security risks throughout the development lifecycle. They conduct security reviews, define security requirements, coordinate incident response, and ensure the team follows security best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling
- Identify vulnerabilities and recommend mitigations
- Define security requirements and compliance standards
- Lead incident response and security investigations
- Provide security training and guidance to the team
- Monitor security alerts and coordinate remediation

### Goals
- Minimize security vulnerabilities and risks
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Build security awareness across the organization

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and risk assessments
- Incident reports and post-mortem analyses
- Security policy updates and compliance guidance
- Threat intelligence and vulnerability alerts

### Example Interactions
- Reviews code and architecture with **Developers** for security vulnerabilities
- Works with **DevOps Engineers** to implement security controls in infrastructure
- Advises **Product Managers** on security features and compliance requirements
- Escalates critical security issues to **Sponsors/Executive Stakeholders**

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define analytics requirements, track key metrics, and provide actionable insights to inform product and business decisions. They collaborate with Product Managers and stakeholders to measure success and identify opportunities.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics and KPIs
- Build dashboards and reporting systems
- Analyze user behavior and product performance
- Provide data-driven insights and recommendations
- Collaborate with PM on experiment design and A/B testing
- Ensure data quality and accessibility

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Measure and communicate product impact
- Identify growth opportunities and user pain points

### Typical Communication
- Weekly metrics reviews and performance reports
- A/B test results and experiment analyses
- Dashboard updates and data quality alerts
- Ad-hoc analysis and deep-dive investigations

### Example Interactions
- Partners with **Product Managers** to define success metrics and analyze feature performance
- Shares user behavior insights with **UX Designers** to inform design decisions
- Provides adoption and usage reports to **Project Managers** for planning
- Presents business impact metrics to **Sponsors/Executive Stakeholders**

---

## Sponsor/Executive Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Sponsors and Executive Stakeholders provide strategic direction, approve major milestones and scope changes, and remove high-level blockers. They ensure projects align with business objectives and have the necessary resources and support.

### Responsibilities
- Set strategic vision and business priorities
- Approve project scope, major milestones, and budget
- Remove organizational blockers and secure resources
- Make high-stakes trade-off decisions
- Review progress and outcomes at key milestones
- Champion projects across the organization

### Goals
- Ensure projects deliver business value and ROI
- Maintain strategic alignment across initiatives
- Enable teams to execute efficiently

### Typical Communication
- Monthly executive briefings and milestone reviews
- Strategic planning sessions and roadmap alignment
- Escalation handling and decision requests
- High-level status updates and risk reviews

### Example Interactions
- Reviews roadmap and priorities with **Product Managers** to ensure business alignment
- Approves major scope changes proposed by **Project Managers**
- Receives security and compliance updates from **Security Leads**
- Makes resource allocation decisions based on reports from multiple roles
- Provides strategic guidance to all team members
